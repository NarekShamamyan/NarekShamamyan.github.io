Creating a Compelling Cover Letter

This microlearning unit is aimed to teach newly graduates how to write a compelling cover letter for prospective job applications. For the design and development of the unit we used Moodle, H5P, Adobe Illustrator, Canva and PowerPoint. The unit follows the Anchored Instruction ID model and has an interactive book format.

The learning unit starts with the story of Lilo, a newly EduTech graduate, who is applying for jobs, but doesn't know how to write a cover letter, so he turns to his friend Ruzanna for help.


After Lilo asks Ruzanna for help, Ruzanna introduces him to the 6 strategies of writing a successful cover letter, and goes over them one by one. Here is a screenshot from the second strategy video: Highlighting your professional skills.


The strategy videos are designed in a way to be more interactive for the learners because the learning unit was created using H5P and hosted on Moodle. So, after each strategy learners get a short example of the strategy and a quiz to test their knowledge. On moodle, that looks like this:


After completing the sections on the six strategies, learners have a short reflection section, then a quiz and a drag and drop exercise, which helps them create a complete cover letter.
