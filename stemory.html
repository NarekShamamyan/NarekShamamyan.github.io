Spark of Stemory

This interactive memory game was designed and developed using Adobe Illustrator, Canva and Godot as part of a Game Design and Programming class

Spark of Stemory is an edutainment-puzzle game, as it is aimed to enhance short-term memory and is embedded in a simple, motivating story. The game has pedagogical objectives: it is divided into five different minigames that are designed for training the short-term memory. At the beginning, players watch a short animation-intro, where they get to know the characters and their backstories. You can find a short snippet of the animation-intro below.

After the player hovers the spaceship near one of the stars, a mini game starts to play. The goal of the players is to make the stars in the sky shine again, which they can do by playing the mini games and winning them.
